Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing Process

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing process ensures consistent quality, optimal material properties, and dimensional accuracy at every production step.

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

We source only the finest PE100 HDPE granules from certified suppliers to ensure consistent quality across every pipe we manufacture.

  • PE100 grade material
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution
  • Carbon black UV stabilisation
  • Third-party material verification

Precision Extrusion Process

State-of-the-art extrusion machinery ensures consistent pipe dimensions and wall thickness throughout production.

  • Temperature-controlled extrusion barrel
  • Real-time outer diameter monitoring
  • Consistent wall thickness
  • Automated quality feedback loop

Controlled Cooling System

Precision cooling systems ensure optimal crystalline structure for maximum pipe strength and flexibility.

  • Vacuum-assisted cooling tanks
  • Precise water-bath temperature control
  • Dimensional stability assurance
  • Stress-relief optimisation

Rigorous Quality Testing

Every batch undergoes comprehensive testing to meet and exceed BIS, ISO and international pipe standards.

  • Hydrostatic pressure testing
  • Impact resistance verification
  • Dimensional accuracy checks
  • Third-party certification

Precision Cutting & Sizing

Automated cutting systems deliver precise pipe lengths with clean, burr-free ends ready for immediate installation.

  • CNC-controlled cutting line
  • Custom length capabilities
  • Automatic coiling for smaller diameters
  • End deburring and finishing

Secure Packaging & Dispatch

Professional packaging ensures your pipes arrive in perfect condition, ready for immediate deployment on site.

  • UV-protected wrapping
  • Bundle strapping
  • Full labelling and traceability
  • Logistics optimisation
